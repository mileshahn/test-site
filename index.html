Study of Albert Bierstadt's Work

Albert Bierstadt was a German-American painter who was known for his lavish landscape painting of the American West. He was one of the most influential artists in the Hudson River School Movement.

A few of Bierstadt's most famous works include:

  • The Last of the Buffalo
  • Mount Adams, Washington
  • A Storm in the Rocky Mountains
  • Valley of the Yosemite
